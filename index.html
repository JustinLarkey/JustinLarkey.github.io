About Me

I'm a rock record producer with a background in filmmaking and a passion for capturing raw energy through sound. I'm studying Recording Arts at Full Sail University and have been producing independently since I was 10, developing a style rooted in punchy guitars, driving rhythms, and honest emotion. My film background taught me the importance of emotion and pacing, and that's what drives my approach to production. I aim to make music that connects, hits hard, and stays real.
